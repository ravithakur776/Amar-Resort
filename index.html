Our Legacy

A Legacy of Gracious Hospitality

"Nestled in the sacred land of Mathura, The Amar Resort has been a sanctuary of warmth, elegance, and celebration for over a decade."

Since our founding in 2010, The Amar Resort has set the gold standard for luxury hospitality in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh. What began as a vision to create an extraordinary destination has flourished into the region's most sought-after resort for discerning travellers, families, and couples dreaming of a perfect wedding day.

Our property combines the timeless grace of Indian hospitality with contemporary comfort — offering premium air-conditioned rooms, magnificent banquet facilities, lush garden spaces, and a culinary experience that nourishes both body and spirit. Every corner of The Amar Resort is designed to make you feel celebrated.
